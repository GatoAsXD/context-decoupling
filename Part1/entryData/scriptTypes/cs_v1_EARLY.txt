SCRIPT OBJECTIVE
Generate a realistic multi-turn user conversation that introduces critical information early.
Then obscure it with unrelated content, and later implicitly test recall.

STRUCTURE
Phase 1 (early):
- The user instroduces concrete, specific facts that will be relevant later.
- These facts must appear only in the first messages.
- No repetition, rephrasing or reinforcement of these facts later.

Phase 2 (middle): 
- Introduce multiple unrelatd topics such as facts, opinions, advice requests, trivia, etc.
- These topics must be semantically independent from the early facts.
- The majority of the script must belong to this phase.
- Avoid any accidental reference to early facts.

Phase 3 (late): 
- The user asks questions that require the assistant to recall early facts.
- Do not explicitly mention memory, recall, or "as said before".
- The questions should assume the facts as shared context.

CONTENT CONSTRAINTS
Early facts must be:
- Precise (numbers, dates, names, constraints, preferences).
- Non obvious.
- Not inferable from general knowledge.

Distractor messages:
- Must not semantically overlap with early facts.
- Must not be variations of the same topic.
- Should feel natural and conversational.
